## Implications for dollar TinyML

If we budget 10-20 cents USD to the microcontroller,
then we get around:

- 16-20 kB FLASH
- 2-3 kB RAM
- 24-48 Mhz clock speed
- 32 bit CPU
- No floating-point unit (FPU)
